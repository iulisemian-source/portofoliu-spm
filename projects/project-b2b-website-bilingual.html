Website Redesign and UX Optimization
Complete website redesign: modern design, mobile-first responsive, 40% faster load time (4.2s ‚Üí 2.1s), clear navigation, prominent CTAs. Strategic Positioning and Service Differentiation
Researched buyer personas and competitive positioning. Repositioned company: \"Strategic technology partner for mid-market growth companies\" not \"technology services vendor.\" Highlighted outcomes: faster time-to-market, 30% cost savings, measurable ROI.
